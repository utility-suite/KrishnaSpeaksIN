A little about me

My name is Krishna Kumar Pandey. I currently work as a Technical Assistant at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru. Most days involve a quiet balance of web development, systems support, and ensuring that research and operations keep moving without friction.

I completed my MCA from JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) and my BCA from Acharya Institute of Graduate Studies. Through these years, I was fortunate to be recognised for academic and extracurricular excellence, but it was the habit of writing, coding, and staying curious that stuck with me most.

My technical work ranges from developing academic portals and dashboards to building custom tools using Angular and the MERN stack. I’ve also contributed to automation workflows and server-side scripting for smoother research operations.

Outside of code, I enjoy mentoring, editing, and collaborating on creative projects. Whether it’s preparing students for exams or helping a team shape their ideas — I find purpose in clear communication and thoughtful execution.

KrishnaSpeaks.IN is where all of that comes together — as notes from the journey, shared one post at a time.
